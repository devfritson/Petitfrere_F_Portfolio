A little about me and my experiences

From the moment I graduated high school, I knew that I wanted to dive straight into the workforce. I started in the food industry, where I worked tirelessly for three years, starting off as a dishwasher and gradually working my way up to becoming a line cook. The experience I gained during this time taught me the importance of hard work and dedication.

But then, I knew it was time for a change. I transitioned into the world of armed and unarmed security, where every day brought a new challenge and an opportunity to learn. The thrill of never knowing what the day would bring kept me motivated and excited to come to work.

And then, just as I was settling into my new role, the Covid-19 pandemic struck. Suddenly, everything was uncertain, and I found myself reevaluating my priorities. It was then that I decided to take a leap and reinvent myself.

I delved into the world of wi-fi hacking and reconnaissance, armed with just a yagi antenna, aircrack-ng, wireshark, and burpsuite. I was relentless in my pursuit of knowledge, constantly deauthing my devices, capturing the four-way handshake, and using dictionary attacks to crack passwords.

But I didn't stop there. I also immersed myself in Linux and Github, learning everything I could about operating systems like Centos, Ubuntu, and Arch Linux. I even got into bash shell scripting, eager to explore new ways to push my limits.

Through all of these experiences, I have learned that growth and progress require taking risks and stepping outside of our comfort zones. And I am proud to say that my efforts have paid off.

I am now CompTIA A + and Security + certified, further demonstrating my commitment to excellence and my dedication to mastering my craft.

I am excited to see what the future holds for me and to continue growing and learning every step of the way
